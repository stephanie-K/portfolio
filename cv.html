Summary


My experience is mainly in the public sector: a local authority, the Scottish Government and the Central Government (Ministry of Justice and the Department for Education).

My projects covered all stages (Discovery, Alpha, Beta and live). I worked in various roles: from frontend developer to interaction design and now service design. On some projects, I worked on the content or user research too.

I have experience of the service standards assessments at every stage, some on the GOV.UK one and others on the Scottish version of it: the Digital Scotland Service Standard (DSSS).

I've used the GOV.UK Design system and its prototype kit which I customised while working in a local authority.

For all projects, I try to be as inclusive as possible and design with accessibility in mind right from the start.
