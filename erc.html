Challenges

  • our Content Management System generates technological constrains which makes recreating some design patterns quite tricky (progressive disclosure, editable summary)
  • we don't have an authentication method yet so without login it's hard to save a form safely
  • the backend systems are minimal for some departments, so the design can't be as simple and efficient without the possibility to retrieve data previously entered
  • tight timing
  • prototyping, user testing and User Centred Design is new to most in the team

My Role

I'm working with a content designer, a digital customer officer and a CRM developer.

  • introducing good practices, GDS ways of working in my team and with stakeholders in various departments
  • raising awareness of accessibility issues, differences between browsers, the need for progressive enhancement and the need to look at the whole service and not just the frontend side
  • facilitate and organise User Research sessions and user testing with various users, including non native English speakers, refugees and homeless participants for various forms
